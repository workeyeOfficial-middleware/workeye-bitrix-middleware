Privacy Policy

WorkEye for Bitrix24

Built for companies using WorkEye — access everything inside Bitrix24, no switching apps

Last updated: March 2026

WorkEye for Bitrix24 is an integration that allows any company using the WorkEye employee monitoring platform to access their WorkEye data directly inside their Bitrix24 portal.

Instead of opening WorkEye separately, your team can view attendance, productivity, activity logs, and screenshots — all from within Bitrix24. Reports can also be synced to your Bitrix24 Drive directly from the app.

This app is designed for businesses and organisations. Each company installs and manages the app independently for their own Bitrix24 portal.

🏢 Who This App Is For

This app is available to any company that:

  • Has an active WorkEye account as an admin
  • Has an active Bitrix24 portal
  • Wants to manage WorkEye data without switching between two platforms

💡 Each company's data is completely separate — one company cannot see another company's data.

📋 What Data We Collect

From Bitrix24 (when your company installs the app):

  • Your Bitrix24 portal ID and domain
  • OAuth access token and refresh token — used to connect to your portal

From WorkEye (when you use the app):

  • Employee names, emails, and positions
  • Attendance records — punch in and punch out times
  • Productivity percentages and screen time
  • Activity logs
  • Screenshots — only when you choose to view or sync them

⚠️ WorkEye data is fetched live from your WorkEye account. We do not permanently store employee data on our servers.

🎯 How We Use Your Data

  • To display your WorkEye data inside your Bitrix24 portal
  • To sync reports to your own Bitrix24 Drive when you request it
  • To keep your session connected between WorkEye and Bitrix24

We never:

  • Sell your data to any third party
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your company's data with other companies
  • Store employee data permanently on our servers

🔐 How We Store Your Data

  • Only your Bitrix24 OAuth tokens are stored so the app stays connected to your portal
  • All data is stored securely with HTTPS encryption
  • Synced reports are saved directly to your Bitrix24 Drive — not on our servers
  • Each company's tokens are stored separately and cannot be accessed by other companies

🗓️ How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Bitrix24 tokens are stored as long as your app is installed
  • When you uninstall the app from Bitrix24, all your tokens are immediately and permanently deleted
  • You can contact us at any time to request deletion of your data

👔 Company Admin Responsibility

This app is installed and managed by your company's Bitrix24 admin. The admin is responsible for:

  • Deciding which employees can access the integration
  • Ensuring employees are informed that WorkEye data is visible in Bitrix24
  • Complying with local employment and privacy laws regarding employee monitoring

💡 WorkEye only displays data your company already has in WorkEye. We do not add any new monitoring capabilities.

🔗 Third Party Services Used

  • Bitrix24 — OAuth authentication and Drive file storage
  • WorkEye — Source of all employee monitoring data
  • Render — Hosting provider where this app runs

Each of these services has their own privacy policy which applies independently.

✅ Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of what data we store for your company
  • Delete — Uninstall the app to instantly delete all stored tokens
  • Correct — Contact us to correct any data
  • Portability — Request your data in a readable format
